More About Oculux
Built by DNet Labs, Oculux facilites the management, distribution, and playback of decentralized video on Jackal Cloud. This project is still in its early phases of development; however, a beta version of both the Oculux Player & Oculux Web is available for use. The Oculux suite will offer various tiers of file security, handle relevant extended metadata, facilitate the development and integration experience, and enables people to effectively view decentralized video from either Web2.0 or Web3.0 environments. As it is still in the early phases of development, there will be missing features, there will be bugs, and there will be questions, all of which will be resolved one step at a time.

The Oculux Player doesn't completely "re-invent the wheel". It works off of video.js which is used by millions of sites and trusted by the world's largest technology companies. Only MP4 and MOV files are eligible for playback which is also the case on most web browsers; however, Oculux plans to offer plugins/extensions that enable other video formats to be played such as AVI and MKV. This can be made possible by transcoding and/or transmuxing streamed data in real-time.
Development & Integration
Oculux Web & Player are entirely open source. The Oculux player is designed to be plug & play for most upcoming or existing websites. Often adding or changing just one line of code is all that's needed in websites that currently use another player such as video.js or the standard HTML player.

Documentation for development & integration will arrive at a later date.
